pyTooling.Cartesian3D.Volumes

An implementation of 3D cartesian volumes for Python.

Classes

  • Volume: Base-class for all 3D cartesian volumes.

  • Cuboid: A cuboid is a volume made out of 6 rectangles.

  • Cube: A cube is a Cuboid made out of 6 equally sized squares.
